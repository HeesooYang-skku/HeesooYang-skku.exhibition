Exhibition Title Language Dashboard

A data visualization project that analyzes repeated words, language types, and conceptual patterns in Korean contemporary art exhibition titles.

This project treats exhibition titles not only as labels, but also as institutional language that shapes the viewer’s first encounter with contemporary art.

Project Focus

Word Frequency

The dashboard shows which words and concepts appear most frequently in exhibition titles.

Language Type

It compares Korean-only, English-only, and Korean-English mixed titles.

Venue Comparison

It compares keyword patterns across museums and galleries to reveal institutional tendencies.

Keyword Categories

Because contemporary art titles often use poetic or abstract language, this project groups related words into broader conceptual categories.

Research Questions

  • What words appear most often in Korean contemporary art exhibition titles?
